# FnP-sales-analysis
An interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard that uncovers key business insights including revenue by occasions, category performance, top cities, and customer ordering trends
# SALES ANALYSIS DASHBOARD REPORT

## 1. Introduction

This document presents a comprehensive analysis of the Sales Dashboard. The dashboard provides key insights into revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and order distribution across different dimensions.

---

## 2. Key Metrics Overview

* **Total Orders:** 1000
* **Total Revenue:** ₹35,20,984
* **Average Order Delivery Time:** 5.53 days
* **Average Customer Spend:** ₹3,520.98

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

---

## 3. Revenue Analysis

### 3.1 Revenue by Occasions

* Highest revenue is generated during **Anniversary** and **Raksha Bandhan** occasions.
* Moderate performance is observed during **Holi** and general occasions.
* Lower revenue contribution from **Valentine's Day** and **Diwali**.

### 3.2 Revenue by Category

* **Soft Toys** generate the highest revenue.
* Followed by **Sweets** and **Colors**.
* **Mugs** contribute the least to total revenue.

---

## 4. Geographic Analysis

### Top Cities by Orders

* **Kavali** leads in order volume.
* Other high-performing cities include **Machilipatnam**, **Tiruchirappalli**, and **Bhubaneswar**.
* Lower order volumes are seen in cities like **North Dumdum** and **Faridabad**.

---

## 5. Time-Based Analysis

### 5.1 Revenue by Months

* Peak revenue observed around **February** and **June**.
* Noticeable dips during **April** and **August**.
* Gradual recovery towards the end of the year.

### 5.2 Revenue by Hour (Order Time)

* High activity observed during early morning and evening hours.
* Peak spikes suggest customer ordering behavior during specific time windows.

---

## 6. Product Performance

1. Magnam Set (Highest revenue contributor)

These products play a crucial role in driving overall sales.

## 7. Filters & Interactivity

The dashboard includes the following filters:

* **Delivery Date (Year-wise)**
* **Order Date (Monthly breakdown)**
* **Occasion filter** (Anniversary, Birthday, Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, Valentine's Day)

These filters allow dynamic data exploration and deeper insights.

## 8. Key Insights

The Sales Analysis Dashboard effectively highlights business performance and trends. It helps in identifying high-performing products, peak sales periods, and key geographic markets. These insights can support data-driven decision-making and future growth strategies.
